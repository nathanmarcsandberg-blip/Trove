What?

We allow you to invest in the same major companies but with better yields than anywhere else. Why should traders and short‑term investors be rewarded when you stick it out with these companies for months or years? You take priority; you take the rewards.

On top of this we’re always finding ways to reward our users more.

How?

To prioritise you against short‑term buyers, we only allow dividends to be shared with users who opt in or “stake” their stocks and have held for at least 60 days. This ensures that with new users joining the platform you will always receive their share of dividends before they reach the 60‑day milestone.

In addition to this, as extra yield and protection against slow user growth, we share all of our treasury yields with our users.

Treasury

The Treasury is our treasure trove! Fifty percent of all fees generated at Trove are put toward growing our crypto treasury. Our entire treasury is staked sporadically across multiple staking pools. All yields received are shared with users to provide extra incentive and reward.

This allows you to benefit from other user actions, including whales or users who joined before you or have stopped using the platform.
